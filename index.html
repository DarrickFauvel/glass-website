About GLASS

The Greater Lowell Area Science-based Skeptics (GLASS) is a group of curious, open-minded individuals who value evidence, reason, and the scientific method. We meet regularly to discuss topics ranging from pseudoscience and the paranormal to medical myths and critical thinking skills.

Whether you're a lifelong skeptic or just starting to question the world around you, GLASS is a welcoming community for anyone who believes that good thinking matters.

  • Science We trust the scientific method as the best tool for understanding the world.
  • Reason We apply logic and critical thinking to evaluate claims and evidence.
  • Community We foster a welcoming space for curious minds of all backgrounds.
